Our Commitment To You

Empowering modern women to feel comfortable and confident through modest clothing that is beautiful, stylish and most importantly practical.

Powered By Ihsaan

From sourcing and production to unparalleled customer service, we uphold the Islamic principle of Ihsaan, emphasizing the pursuit of beauty and excellence in everything we do.

Next Day Delivery

We understand when ordering a piece of clothing, time may be of the essence! All of our pieces are dispatched via Royal Mail first class the next working day, providing you order before our cut-off time of 12pm.

Ethical Modelling

Modest Fashion Done Right

At ZAYNAH, we believe it is possible to model in a dignified manner following Islamic principles of modesty. However, in practice, the modest fashion industry rarely gets it right. The portrayal of unrealistic beauty standards, immodest poses, and the emphasis on certain physical attributes of the model sadly still exist. Read on to learn more about our approach to ethical modelling.
